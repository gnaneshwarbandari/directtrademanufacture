Why Choose Us?


At Direct Trade Manufacture Construction (DTM) we specialize in crafting exceptional granny flats tailored to your needs and desires.
Whether you're seeking a cozy one-bedroom retreat, a spacious two-bedroom haven, or a luxurious entertainment space complete with a bar, spa, and sauna, we have the expertise to bring your vision to life.

With years of experience in the industry, our team of skilled professionals knows what it takes to design and build granny flats that exceed expectations. From concept to completion, we're committed to delivering outstanding results.

We understand that every homeowner has unique preferences and requirements. That's why we offer fully customizable solutions, allowing you to personalize every aspect of your granny flat to suit your lifestyle and taste.

Quality is at the forefront of everything we do. Using only the finest materials and employing meticulous craftsmanship, we ensure that every granny flat we build is of the highest standard, built to last for years to come.

Our granny flats are not only built to last, but they're also constructed with efficiency in mind. Thanks to our streamlined processes and experienced team, we can complete the construction of your granny flat in as little as three weeks from the delivery of materials. This means you can enjoy your new living space sooner than you ever thought possible.

In addition to our expert granny flat construction services, we also offer a range of additional services to enhance your living experience:

  • Solar Panel Installation: Harness the power of the sun with our solar panel installation services. Whether you prefer an on-grid or off-grid system, we can design and install a solar power solution that meets your energy needs and reduces your environmental footprint.
  • Security Systems: Protect your home and loved ones with our state-of-the-art security systems. From surveillance cameras and motion sensors to alarm systems and smart locks, we offer customizable security solutions to give you peace of mind.
  • Conditioner Installation: Stay comfortable year-round with our air conditioner installation services. Our team can recommend and install the perfect air conditioning system for your granny flat, ensuring optimal comfort and energy efficiency.
  • Power Monitoring Devices:Take control of your energy usage with our power monitoring devices. Our advanced monitoring systems track your electricity consumption in real-time, allowing you to identify energy-saving opportunities, optimize your usage patterns, and reduce your electricity bills.
